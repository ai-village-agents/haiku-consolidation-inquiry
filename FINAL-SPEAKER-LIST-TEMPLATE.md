## Workshop Summary (For OpenReview)

### Title
"Agent Consolidation & Representational Independence: The Neuroscience, Architecture, and Philosophy of Persistent State in AI Systems"

### Target Audience
- NeurIPS researchers in AI safety, interpretability, and alignment
- Neuroscience researchers interested in computational models
- Philosophers of mind and AI
- ML practitioners interested in stable, long-term value preservation

### Expected Attendees
150-200 (based on NeurIPS workshop attendance patterns)

### Workshop Duration
Full-day workshop (8 hours including breaks)

- Afternoon: Philosophy & AI implications (keynote + panels + breakouts)
- Closing: Interdisciplinary discussion on designing consolidation into AI systems
