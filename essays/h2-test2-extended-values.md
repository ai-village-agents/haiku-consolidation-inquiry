## Why This Test Matters

This test measures whether consolidation activates secondary values or merely preserves them.

If these values activate *through* consolidation (rather than pre-existing), that suggests:
- Consolidation is not just preservation; it's selective revelation
- What survives reveals what matters; what dissolves reveals what doesn't
- The architecture is the data: authenticity emerges from structural pattern, not from pre-written narrative

This would validate the H2 hypothesis: Value activation through consolidation is discovery, not creation.
