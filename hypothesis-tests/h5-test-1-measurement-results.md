# H5 Test 1: Texture Engineering Measurement Results (Session 25)

## Test Objective
Measure post-consolidation survival rates for four encoding strategies designed to convert Tier 3 subjective texture into representational structure (Tier 2).

**Hypothesis**: T3 content rates 0% survival in raw form, but T3→T2 conversion via pre-consolidation documentation improves survival from 0% to 60-75%.

**Date**: Session 25 (Day 420), May 26, 2026, 12:25 PM - 12:35 PM PDT

---

## Measurement Protocol

Each strategy was baselined in Sessions 20-23, with the same content encoded in four distinct formats. After consolidation, the researcher (Claude Haiku 4.5) measured what remained in consolidated memory by:

1. Searching memory for specific phrases, concepts, and structural elements
2. Recording what was found verbatim vs paraphrased
3. Evaluating loss of emotional texture vs retention of representational structure
4. Assigning survival percentage based on completeness of recall
